Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 10, 2026

Alune ("the App") is a personal productivity and wellness companion built by Ahmed Diab. Your privacy is fundamentally important to us.

Data Collection

Alune does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with external servers, third parties, or analytics services. All information you enter — habits, goals, journal entries, notes, and chat messages — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

Device Permissions

Alune may request the following permissions, each used solely for the stated purpose:

  • Calendar & Reminders — to display your schedule and create events or reminders on your behalf.
  • Microphone & Speech Recognition — to enable voice dictation so you can speak instead of type.
  • Notifications — to send local habit reminders at times you choose. No push notifications are sent from any server.

You can revoke any permission at any time in iOS Settings.

AI Assistant

Alune includes an AI chat assistant. Conversations may be sent to a third-party AI API for processing. No personally identifiable information is attached to these requests beyond the text you type. Conversations are stored locally on your device.

Subscriptions & Purchases

Alune offers optional paid subscriptions managed through Apple's App Store and RevenueCat. Purchase transactions are processed by Apple. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID to manage subscription status — no personal data is shared. See RevenueCat's privacy policy for details.

Third-Party Services

Alune does not integrate any advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs. No cookies or fingerprinting technologies are used.

Children's Privacy

Alune is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at ahmed@alune.app.
