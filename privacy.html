Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 1, 2026

Alune ("the App") is a personal productivity and wellness companion built by Ahmed Diab. Your privacy is fundamentally important to us.

On-Device Data

The personal data you create in Alune — habits, goals, journal entries, notes, and calendar or reminder references — is stored on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is not uploaded to any server.

Data Sent to Servers

Certain features require network communication. When you use these features, limited data leaves your device as described below:

  • AI Assistant (text): When you send a message to the AI assistant, the text of your conversation is transmitted over HTTPS to a secure server-side proxy hosted on Supabase, which forwards it to a third-party AI model provider (OpenRouter) for processing. No personally identifiable information beyond the text you type is attached. Conversations are not stored on any server after processing.
  • Voice transcription: When you use the voice input feature, your audio recording is transmitted over HTTPS to the same Supabase proxy, which forwards it to OpenAI's Whisper service for speech-to-text conversion. The audio is not retained after transcription.
  • Subscription management: Alune uses RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat receives your anonymous App Store purchase receipts to verify subscription status. No personal data such as your name or email is shared with RevenueCat. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Device Permissions

Alune may request the following permissions, each used solely for the stated purpose:

  • Calendar & Reminders — to display your schedule and create events or reminders on your behalf.
  • Microphone — to enable voice dictation so you can speak instead of type.
  • Notifications — to send local habit reminders at times you choose. No push notifications are sent from any server.

You can revoke any permission at any time in iOS Settings.

AI Assistant Limits

  • Free trial: If you do not have an active subscription or AI add-on, you may send a limited number of Assistant messages (shown in the app) as a one-time trial. After that, Assistant requires a qualifying purchase.
  • Pro subscribers: Assistant is included with up to 300 messages per day (or the limit shown in the app).
  • We may adjust daily limits for security, stability, or fair use. Current limits are always described in the app.

Data Retention & Deletion

All personal data (habits, goals, journal entries, notes, medicine logs, and reading lists) is stored exclusively on your device. Alune does not maintain any server-side copy of this data.

  • Delete all data: To delete all your data, simply delete the Alune app from your device. All locally stored data will be removed immediately.
  • AI conversations: Message text sent to the AI assistant is not retained on any server after processing. Conversation history stored on your device is deleted when you delete the app.
  • Voice recordings: Audio sent for transcription is discarded by the transcription service immediately after processing and is not stored.
  • Subscription data: Anonymous purchase receipts held by RevenueCat are subject to RevenueCat's data retention policy. You may contact RevenueCat directly to request deletion of your anonymous receipt data.

To request deletion of any data associated with your use of Alune, contact us at alunesupport@gmail.com.

Third-Party Services

Alune does not integrate any advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs. No cookies or fingerprinting technologies are used. The only third-party services are those described above (AI processing via OpenRouter/OpenAI and subscription management via RevenueCat).

Children's Privacy

Alune is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at alunesupport@gmail.com.
